FOOD FOR ALL - FAQ
Q: What is FOOD FOR ALL?
A: FOOD FOR ALL is a surplus food management platform dedicated to reducing food waste and addressing hunger by connecting surplus food providers with those in need.
Q: How can I donate surplus food?
A: Donating surplus food is easy! You can register as a donor on our platform and follow the simple steps to contribute surplus food from your business or organization.
Q: Can individuals get involved?
A: Absolutely! Individuals can contribute by volunteering, spreading awareness, or supporting us financially. Visit our "Get Involved" section for more details.
